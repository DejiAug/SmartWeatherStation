About

Smart Weather Station is designed to track the weather of your local environment in a smart way that is easy and well convenient for you.

It uses several different sensors, they are; Anemometer for Wind speed, Humidity sensor, Temperature sensor, Raindrops sensor and an Air pressure sensor, all constructed together in the Arduino code along with a Bluetooth module that makes it amazingly comfortable for you to track the weather data from your smartphone.

The Smart Weather Station uses a webpage constructed by HTML and CSS styling that is very responsive, which means the layout of the webpage changes when you make the browser smaller or bigger.
